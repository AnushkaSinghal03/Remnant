Remnant is an archive of old books. These books serve as a reminder of our history and our rich culture. Whether shaped by changing weather, difficult times, or the quiet struggles of poverty, every genre and every voice deserves to be remembered. We aim to honor them all.

Our collection may be small for now, but for a 12-year-old reader, it could become the very thing that sparks a lifelong love for literature. In our fast-paced world, we must learn to adapt alongside technology. As printed books become increasingly expensive, we turn to these older works that still deserve our love, attention, and remembrance.

All the books available in Remnant are believed to be in the public domain in India. If any book is found to still be under copyright unintentionally, please contact us with appropriate proof. Every title has been cross-checked multiple times; however, we are only human and intend no infringement.

The book covers displayed on our platform are our own original creations and remain our property.

Since we provide only public-domain books, this platform operates on a non-profit basis. Any donations you may wish to offer are deeply appreciated and help support the maintenance and continued operation of our site.
